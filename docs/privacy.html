Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 6, 2025

Timeshare Calculators respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the app.

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. All data entered into the app (e.g., purchase prices, vacation costs) stays entirely on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

2. Usage Analytics

We use a third-party service, Amplitude, to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. This includes non-personal information such as:

  • Which features are used
  • How often the app is opened
  • Device type (e.g., iPhone, iPad)
  • App version

This data is collected without identifying you personally and is used solely to understand how the app is used and improve functionality.

3. No Internet Requirement

The app works offline and does not require an internet connection to function. Usage analytics are sent only when your device is online.

4. No Account or Login

You do not need to create an account or log in to use the app. We do not authenticate users in any way.
